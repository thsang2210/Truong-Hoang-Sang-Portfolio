🏆 Round 3 Case: EcoPipe Australia Decarbonization

- 🏢 Context: EcoPipe Australia is a leading supplier of piping systems with a national logistics network. The company aims to reduce its carbon footprint and transition its fleet to electric vehicles (EVs) as part of its decarbonization strategy, EcoMove 2025.

- 🎯 Objective: Evaluate the current logistics network, calculate costs and CO2 emissions, and develop strategies to reduce emissions through EV adoption and network optimization.

- 📜 Requirement:

  • Develop a 3-year Demand Forecast for each product category.
  • Calculate current logistics costs and CO2 emissions from the diesel-powered network.
  • Design an optimal logistics network to minimize costs and CO2 emissions.
  • Propose a 3-5 year roadmap for transitioning to an electric vehicle fleet.

🏆 Round 2 Case: VIBRABREW Decarbonization

- 🏢 Context: VIBRABREW Co. is a major brewing company in Vietnam with multiple production sites. The company is aiming to achieve net-zero emissions by 2040 and focuses on decarbonizing its inbound logistics and warehousing operations.

- 🎯 Objective: Analyze carbon emissions from VIBRABREW's inbound logistics and warehousing, propose decarbonization solutions, and reduce carbon footprint.

- 📜 Requirement:

  • Calculate GHG (Greenhouse Gas) emissions using activity-based methods for inbound logistics and fuel-based methods for warehousing.
  • Identify emission hotspots and propose short-term operational and long-term strategic decarbonization actions.
